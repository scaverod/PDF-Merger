How to Use PDF Merger

1

Upload Main PDFs

Drag and drop one or more PDF files into the "Main PDFs" section, or click to browse and select them.

2

Upload Cover/Back PDF

Drag and drop a single PDF into the "PDF to Insert" section. This will be your cover or back page.

3

Choose Position

Select whether to insert the PDF at the First Page (cover) or Last Page (back page).

4

Merge & Download

Click the "Merge PDFs" button. A ZIP file with all your processed PDFs will be ready to download.
